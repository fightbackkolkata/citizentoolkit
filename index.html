Defend Our Streets

Stop the Eviction of Hawkers in Kolkata!

We are at a critical juncture where the livelihoods of our fellow hawkers in Kolkata are under threat. The recent eviction orders targeting these hardworking individuals undermine not just their right to earn a living but also the vibrant character of our city. As concerned citizens, it is our duty to stand in solidarity against such unjust measures.

Raise Awareness

Social Media Campaigns

Use platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram to share stories of street vendors, highlight their contributions to our community, and spread awareness about the eviction issue.

Raise Awarenes

Write letters to the editor of local newspapers, call in to radio shows, or submit op-eds advocating for the rights of street vendors and urging reconsideration of eviction policies.
